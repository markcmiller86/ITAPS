SciDAC CETs

Because meshing and discretization pervades many aspects of PDE solutions, we must interact with a broad spectrum of other tool providers to ensure that our software and interfaces are both flexible and efficient on terascale computers. To accomplish this goal, we are committed to working with other SciDAC Centers for Enabling Technologies (CETs). In particular, in SciDAC-1 we interacted strongly with two CETs:

  • TOPS: The Terascale Optimal PDE Simulations (TOPS) PI: David Keyes (ODU), ITAPS Point of Contact: M. Shephard (RPI)

  • CCA: Center for Component Technology for Terascale Simulation Software PI: David Bernholdt (ORNL), ITAPS Point of Contact: L. Diachin (LLNL)

In SciDAC-2, we will continue our strong interactions with these two CETs and begin interacting with two new centers:

  • VACET: The Visulization and Analytics Center for Enabling Technologies (VACET) PI: Wes Bethel (LBNL), ITAPS Point of Contact: Mark Miller (LLNL)

  • CSCAPES: PI: Alex Pothen (Old Dominion), ITAPS Point of Contact: K. Devine (SNL)
